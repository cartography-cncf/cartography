# Commercial Support Listing Criteria

The Commercial Support section lists companies where you can get help with Cartography.

## Criteria

1. Offer **Cartography-specific** consulting, training, or deployment support.
1. Provide these services **independently**: a customer can engage you for Cartography help without purchasing a separate product.
1. Have a **public page** describing their Cartography offerings.

## What belongs elsewhere

Products that use Cartography as an internal component — where Cartography
expertise is bundled into a different product offering — are valued members
of the ecosystem and belong in the ["Who uses Cartography?"](README.md#who-uses-cartography) section.

The distinction: *Can I buy Cartography support from you without buying
something else?*
